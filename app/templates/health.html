Hey there!

SmarSole was created to empower runners to better visualize their gait. We can only push our boundaries to the limit, if we understand them first. In the world of competitive athletics - small changes are everything. Our device provides users a granular view of their weight distribution as they step so they can make those changes one step at a time. Our dashboard will provide users with important metrics: a heat map visualization of their foot correlated with pressure exerted in newtons, a pronation status, the length of your run in seconds, and finally the length of a single step.
